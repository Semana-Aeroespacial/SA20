The aerospace Week is an event organized by AeroTéc, the center of aerospace Engineering students, where the main goal is to enable IST students to contact the companies in the aeronautical/aerospace sector.

With this event it is intended, in addition to the exchange of knowledge with each of the companies present, that students can gather information and get opportunities to perform internships and theses, for example.

In this event we try to promote diversity and inclusion, whilst also considering the sustainability of the planet.

For Students:

- Contact with leading companies.

- Leactures by relevant speakers.

For corporations:

- Contact with brilliant students.

- Advertisement.
